User Data Deletion Agreement

This page provides a deletion agreement template you can display on your website to explain how users may request deletion of their personal data and what we (the data controller) will do in response.

1. Scope & Definitions

Data Controller
The organization operating this website ("Organization").
Personal Data
Any information that identifies or could reasonably identify an individual, including name, email, account identifiers, profile data, and usage logs.
Deletion Request
A user's request that the Organization permanently erase Personal Data associated with the user.

2. What you can request

  • Complete deletion of your Personal Data from active systems and databases.
  • Removal of your profile, account, and content posted by you (subject to exceptions below).
  • Confirmation that the deletion was completed or a reasoned refusal if we cannot comply.

3. How to make a deletion request

Fill the form below or contact privacy@example.org. We may request verification to confirm the identity of the requester before processing.

Note: We may ask for additional proof if necessary. Files uploaded are used solely for verification and will be deleted after processing unless otherwise required by law.

4. Our obligations & timelines

  1. Acknowledgement: We will acknowledge receipt within 5 business days.
  2. Verification: If identity verification is required, we will request it within 5 business days.
  3. Deletion: We will complete deletion from active systems within 30 days of verification, unless an exception applies.
  4. Backups: Copies in offline backups may persist for up to 90 days and will be deleted in accordance with our backup retention and recovery procedures.
Exceptions: We may refuse or partially fulfill deletion requests when necessary to: comply with legal obligations (tax, law enforcement, audits), defend legal claims, complete transactions you requested, or preserve anonymized/aggregated statistical data.

5. Scope of deletion & residual data

Deletion removes personal identifiers and account data from our active systems. Some derived data (aggregated analytics or logs irreversibly anonymized) may be retained for legitimate business purposes.

6. Proof of deletion

Upon completion we will provide a confirmation email describing which data was deleted and the effective deletion date. We do not provide access to internal audit logs, but can provide a deletion certificate on request if available.

7. Dispute & escalation

If you disagree with our decision, please contact privacy@example.org. You may escalate to relevant supervisory authorities under applicable law.

8. Miscellaneous

  • This agreement is a template and does not constitute legal advice. Consult your legal counsel to adapt to local laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA, PDPA).
  • Governing law: Specify jurisdiction (e.g., State/Country where your organization is established).
